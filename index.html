About

Welcome to Beau's Bonbons! A family run store provinding all of your candy needs!

Yes, shrubberies are my trade—I am a shrubber. My name is Roger the Shrubber. I arrange, design, and sell shrubberies.

Candy

We sell a variety of candies ranging from sweet to sour, crunchy to chewy, hard to gummy and everything in between. Be sure to take time to check out all our store has to offer! You're in for a treat!

  • Grassy shrubs

    Grassy shrubs are pretty much grass, but I group them together in clumps so they look kinda like shrubs.

  • Shrubs with laurels

    Shrubs with laurels have pretty little flowers that bloom sometime during the summer. They’re nice.

  • Two-level shrubbery

    Two level shrubbery is where I put two shrubs at different heights, usually with a little path running down the middle.
